Thiruvananthapuram

Thiruvananthapuram also called Trivandrum is the largest city in Kerala. A city is a beautiful place and it has many things that one can do in this city. My city Trivandrum is also a very big hub of Information technology and it has given a lot of engineers and IT’s to the city and the country at large. The technopark is also the largest technology park in Asia. The city is also the greenest city and it has many things that one can do in this city. As Mahatma Gandhi said about this city that it is one of the evergreen cities of the country and Trivandrum maintained that and it till date is very green and also the cleanest. The city isn’t located in the coastal area and it has greenery and fresh air all the time. My city Trivandrum also has the richest temple in the world and the temple is called Padmanabhaswamy Temple. A lot of people come and worship in this temple and it is constructed in a very beautiful manner and is very nice. The people of Trivandrum speaks in Malayalam and it is their official language. The people celebrate all the functions with joy and excitement. The city also holds a Guinness world record of holding the Attukal Pongala drawing. The international film festival of Kerala is also one of the most awaited festivals in Kerala. The city has many colleges and people from across the country comes and study in these colleges. The people of Trivandrum are very educated and they have the highest number of literate people. Thiruvananthapuram also organizes sports and events that are acclaimed nationally and internationally. Kovalam and Varkala are amongst the most popular beaches in Trivandrum. The Agasthyamaia Biosphere Reserve is the also in the list of UNESCO. The city has a unique style of architecture and it is influenced by the British and the Dravidian culture.
